Search for Cheap Holidays with Teletext Holidays

A holiday is that much sweeter when you’ve got yourself a deal that won’t play havoc with your budget. However, smart saving doesn’t mean you have to skimp on the comfort factor. We do the homework and help you find cheap holidays across the board, whether it’s relaxing at the beach, indulging in a spa vacation or enjoying the buzz of a city break.

Traditionally, a cheap holiday is found in the non-touristy seasons or when the school holidays are over, but we know that may not suit your calendar. If you book right, you can find great early bird discounts as well as last minute deals in peak season, too. If you believe in travelling smart, you can also choose to book now, pay later, which means you put down a small deposit now and pay the rest in the days before you travel, reducing the burden on your pocket and giving you time to save up for those all-important ice creams!

For over 28 years Teletext Holidays has strived to offer our customers great value holidays to all of their favourite destinations including Spain, Turkey, Greece and beyond. Destinations such as Benidorm, Tenerife and Lanzarote offer exotic beaches and balmy weather without flying too far, or if you want to go the extra mile, city locations such as glamorous Dubai are sure to satisfy – and you won’t have to spend a fortune to get there.
